‘Karuppu’ team to modify Ilaiyaraaja dialogue: No intention to disrespect maestro, says production house

The makers of Karuppu have said that they will “remove or modify” the dialogue referring to legendary music composer Ialiyaraaja. Starring Suriya, the Tamil movie is directed by RJ Balaji. ‘Karuppu’ movie review: Suriya enters ‘God Mode’ in RJ Balaji’s masala fanfare On May 19, 2026 (Tuesday), production house Dream Warrior Pictures took to X to inform fans that the film had “no intention to offend, hurt or disrespect him.” In the movie, which released on May 15, 2026 (Friday), a dialogue referes to Ilaiyaraaja’s strict actions towards unauthorised use or remixing of his songs. Dream Warrior Pictures said in the post, “We understand that a particular dialogue appearing in Karuppu has caused concern and has upset him. We sincerely regret that the dialogue has resulted in such sentiments.” The production house further wrote, “We deeply value and respect his feelings and as a gesture of our regard, we have decided to remove/modify the concerned portion in future versions and subsequent exploitations of the film.” The film team said that the “reference in the movie was part of a broader satirical context and was not conceived as a personal remark directed at him.” Meanwhile, Karuppu has enjoyed a terrific opening at the box office. On May 18, 2026 (Monday), the production house announced that the movie has grossed ₹147 crore worldwide in the opening weekend.
